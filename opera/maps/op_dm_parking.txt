Name: Parking Garage
Designer: Ross Mansfield
Type: Opera DM
#Players: 2-6

Description:
In the heart of the Hong Kong industrial district,
This car park is used by day for office workers but 
by night, a far more sinister side materialises.  
Illegal firearms and drugs deals are done in its 
dark depths and someone is sure to get a bad deal...
