Name: Shadowy Industry
Designer: Yamazaki (AKA Brian Dorner)
Type: Opera Deathmatch
Players: 2-6

Description:
Day or night, business is conducted in the criminal underworld around the 
clock. Sometimes the arrangements don't go as planned and the deals go 
sour. It happens all the time, and tonight its happening here. This 
processing plant normally breaks down raw materials, but now it will 
seperate the quick from the dead.
